Why It Works

Water that behaves differently on contact

Kinetic Water reaches into micropores and textured surfaces that standard water bridges over. It breaks the bond between soil and surface. Then it holds what it has lifted, keeping it suspended until the machine takes it away.

No re-deposition. No residue. No film. There is nothing to dose, nothing to dilute, nothing to rinse. It is still water. It just works harder than any water you have used before.
